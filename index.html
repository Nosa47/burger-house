Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Chef Michael Reynolds, Burger House began with a simple mission: to create the perfect burger using only the finest ingredients sourced from local farms and producers.

What started as a small food truck has grown into one of the city's most beloved burger destinations, known for our commitment to quality, flavor, and exceptional dining experience.

Every burger is handcrafted with care, from our house-ground premium beef blends to our freshly baked artisanal buns and signature sauces made from scratch daily.

Michael Reynolds

Executive Chef & Founder
